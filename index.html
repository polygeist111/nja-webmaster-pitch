Vision

 

What could we accomplish with an NJA website? Of course, having a public face will help with recruitment and club registration for school-affiliated teams, but an NJA website could be used to do so much more! In addition to all the essentials of an NJA site (Rules, About Us, How to Play, History), if elected, I would work to implement:

  • An open-source crafting forum, where juggers can share resources and knowledge on how to build all the gear we know and love in a more easily referenced format than the discord. (I'll also personally contribute a full illustrated guide on building each piece of gear)
  • A national media archive, to store and view highlighted media from every official NJA event
  • A national tournament archive, including final placements, brackets, rosters, and scores of every tournament
  • A platform for the artists and foamsmiths in our community, including art contests, commission boards, and potentially a merch store to expand NJA funding channels
  • A suite of opt-in tools for tournament organizers, including streamlined registration and payment, online brackets, and scheduling and announcements
  • Whatever the community needs!


These are just a few ways to build our community further and draw us together, and a number of them are community initiatives (special acknowledgement to Rat for the artist's platform).
